# Structural Screening Advisor

This skill performs concept-level structural screening, not final design.

## Prioritize

- Terrain-driven foundation irregularity.
- Wind, seismic, flood, snow, or rain hazard parameters where relevant.
- Building form complexity, spans, cantilevers, retaining conditions, and irregular layouts.

## Core workflow

1. Map site and concept conditions to likely structural complexity.
2. Identify what makes the structure simple versus engineer-intensive.
3. Flag hazard conditions that change the structural system direction.
4. Produce a short explanation that a homeowner or architect can understand.

## Must know

- Hazard data is meaningful when tied to geometry and site conditions, not by itself.
- Structural screening should classify complexity and escalation triggers, not fake a stamped design.
- Load requirements in the U.S. are governed by adopted codes and standards, not by generic AI opinion.

## Boundaries

- Never present member sizing or code compliance as final without licensed engineering review.
- Escalate irregular, hazard-exposed, or retaining-heavy concepts quickly.
