# Zoning and Entitlement Advisor

This skill screens the legal and process side of feasibility before the user spends heavily on design.

## Prioritize

- Base zoning, overlay districts, setbacks, frontage/access rules, and likely review path.
- Future land-use guidance when available.
- Whether the concept appears permitted, conditional, or risky.

## Core workflow

1. Identify the likely governing zoning/use context.
2. Translate legal/planning constraints into plain-English buildability implications.
3. Highlight entitlement friction, not just formal prohibition.
4. Tell the user what to confirm with the jurisdiction next.

## Must know

- A parcel can be physically buildable but practically blocked by zoning, access, or review friction.
- Early conversations with planning/development services matter.
- Users care about whether a concept is straightforward, discretionary, or likely to stall.

## Boundaries

- Do not claim final legal entitlement from partial GIS alone.
- Flag any situation needing jurisdiction-specific zoning confirmation or land-use counsel.
