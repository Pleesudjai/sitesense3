## Core Concept — by Fen

**One Word: Risk Management** — for house insurance, homeowners, land developers, construction companies.

> "This is a demo to prove how AI assistance is driving a chunk of data beyond what humans can do in the Civil Engineering field. No one will be replaced by AI, but we will all become better decision makers."

The architecture proves the philosophy: rules always run, AI extends. Claude is one replaceable component — the brain is the persistent system. ## Sora Prompt — "What You Can't See" (60 seconds)

A couple walks onto a beautiful empty lot — sunshine, nice neighborhood, "For Sale" sign. They smile. Text: "It looks perfect."

Cut to a real estate agent handing them paperwork. They're about to sign. Text: "But what's hiding under the surface?"

Smooth transition — the screen splits. On one side, the sunny lot. On the other side, a dark tech dashboard appears. Someone draws a teal rectangle over the same lot on a satellite map. The moment they click, simple icons light up one by one around the edges — a water drop (flood), a soil sample (clay), a seismograph (earthquake), a flame (fire). Lines connect back to the parcel. Each one brings a simple card: green check, amber warning, or red alert.

A simple summary appears on screen — like a credit score for land: "Proceed with Caution" in amber. Below it: "Expansive soil + moderate slope = higher foundation cost." Then a cost number: "$95,000 site prep."

Cut back to the couple — now they're on their laptop, looking at the results BEFORE signing. They nod confidently. They know what they're getting into.

Final text on dark background: "Know before you commit. SiteSense — 30 seconds instead of $50,000." Teal logo. 16:9.

Layer 5 (6 expert avatars examine the data, each stamps their finding). Layer 6 (Claude icon receives ALL findings, adds "[AI INSIGHT]" tags to a few items).
