# Hack Pitch AI Note

## Core message

SiteSense is not just a GIS dashboard and not just a chatbot.

It is an AI-guided feasibility system that combines:

- raw GIS layers
- deterministic engineering calculations
- cost and hazard indicators
- synthetic domain experts
- professional-review handoff

## Why this matters

A traditional web app can show maps, numbers, and fixed rules.

SiteSense uses AI for the harder part:

- synthesizing many signals into one judgment
- explaining tradeoffs in plain language
- adapting the output for homeowners, architects, and small developers
- identifying what is still unknown
- preparing a clean next-step brief for real engineers

## One-line pitch

SiteSense turns fragmented parcel data into an AI-guided early feasibility decision, so users can understand buildability, risk, and likely next steps before spending thousands on full design work.

## Strong judge-facing framing

What makes this meaningfully AI is that the system does not just render maps.
It behaves like a coordinated team of synthetic experts working over structured evidence:

- parcel strategist
- foundation advisor
- stormwater reviewer
- site design advisor
- cost forecaster
- data quality auditor

That lets the product do something a traditional dashboard struggles to do well:
produce a usable judgment, explain why, and surface uncertainty honestly.

## Technical credibility line

The AI is not used as a freeform calculator.
It sits on top of code-based geometry, GIS overlap analysis, risk scoring, and cost logic, then synthesizes the result into user-ready and engineer-ready outputs.

## Ethical credibility line

SiteSense is preliminary planning support, not permit-ready design.
The system is built to flag uncertainty and hand work off to licensed professionals rather than pretending to replace them.

## Suggested slide bullets

- `Traditional tools:` show layers, tables, and raw reports
- `SiteSense:` reasons across data sources and explains what they mean
- `Traditional tools:` leave users to interpret technical conflicts
- `SiteSense:` synthesizes risks, tradeoffs, and next steps
- `Traditional tools:` stop at analysis
- `SiteSense:` produces owner-facing guidance and engineer-facing handoff

## Suggested demo sentence

"The AI here is acting like an early feasibility copilot. It reads the structured site evidence, routes that evidence through specialist experts, and turns it into a decision that a homeowner can understand and an engineer can review."
